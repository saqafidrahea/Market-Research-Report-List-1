<p><ul><li>Wire Wound Type</li><li>Laminated Type</li><li>Braided Type</li><li>Thin Film Chip Inductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive SMD power inductors market features several types that cater to different applications. Wire wound types utilize insulated wire wound around a magnetic core for efficient energy storage. Laminated types employ thin magnetic layers, reducing eddy current losses, ideal for high-frequency applications. Braided types consist of multiple strands to enhance current handling and reduce inductance, suitable for compact designs. Thin film chip inductors are precision-engineered for minimal size and optimal performance, often used in advanced automotive electronics.</p></p>
