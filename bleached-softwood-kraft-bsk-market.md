<p><p>Bleached Softwood Kraft (BSK) refers to a type of pulp derived from softwood trees, undergoing a bleaching process to enhance brightness and purity. The market is primarily segmented into Southern Bleached Softwood Kraft (SBSK) and Northern Bleached Softwood Kraft (NBSK). SBSK, produced mainly from Southern pine species, is valued for its high strength and brightness, ideal for products like paper and tissue. NBSK, made from Northern softwoods, offers superior fiber length and is preferred for applications requiring high-quality printing and packaging.</p></p>
