<p><strong>The Medical Serration Balloon Catheter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>OTW Serration Balloon Catheter</li><li>Rapid Exchange Serration Balloon Catheter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Serration Balloon Catheter market comprises two primary types: Over-the-Wire (OTW) Serration Balloon Catheters and Rapid Exchange Serration Balloon Catheters. OTW Serration Balloon Catheters allow for the passage of a guidewire through the catheter, offering enhanced maneuverability and control during procedures. In contrast, Rapid Exchange Serration Balloon Catheters feature a design where the guidewire exits through a distal port, enabling quicker exchanges and facilitating faster interventions. Both types are crucial for various medical applications, particularly in minimally invasive surgeries.</p></p>
