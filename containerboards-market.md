<p><p>The containerboards market primarily consists of two types: linerboard and corrugating medium. Linerboard is the flat, exterior layer that provides structural strength and printability for packaging. Corrugating medium, on the other hand, is the fluted, inner layer that adds flexibility and cushioning, allowing for lightweight yet durable packaging solutions. Together, these components form corrugated boxes, which are widely used in shipping and storage, catering to various industries such as e-commerce, food, and consumer goods.</p></p>
