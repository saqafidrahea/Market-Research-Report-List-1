<p><p>The UHF RFID tag inlays market consists of two primary types: UHF dry inlays and UHF wet inlays. UHF dry inlays are typically used in applications requiring high durability, such as retail tags or logistics, featuring a sturdy construction that doesn’t require additional adhesive. In contrast, UHF wet inlays are designed for integration into products or packaging during manufacturing, often featuring a flexible design for easier attachment. Both types cater to diverse industry needs, enhancing tracking and identification efficiency.</p></p>
