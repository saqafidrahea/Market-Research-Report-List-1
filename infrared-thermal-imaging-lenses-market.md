<p><p>The infrared thermal imaging lenses market is categorized mainly into spheres, aspheres, and other types. Spherical lenses have a consistent curvature, making them effective for focusing infrared light, while aspheric lenses feature varying curvature profiles to minimize optical aberrations, improving image quality in thermal applications. Other types may include specialized lenses designed for specific uses, incorporating innovative materials or technologies. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing thermal imaging systems used in fields like security, medical diagnostics, and industrial monitoring.</p></p>
