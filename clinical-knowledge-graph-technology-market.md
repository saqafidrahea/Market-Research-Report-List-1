<p><p>Clinical Knowledge Graph Technology encompasses two main market types: structured and unstructured knowledge graphs. Structured knowledge graphs organize data in a well-defined format, utilizing schemas and ontologies to represent relationships between clinical concepts, ensuring consistency and ease of querying. Conversely, unstructured knowledge graphs deal with raw data, such as clinical notes and research articles, which require natural language processing to extract insights. Both types enhance decision-making in healthcare by linking diverse medical information for improved patient outcomes.</p></p>
