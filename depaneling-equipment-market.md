<p><p>The depaneling equipment market is divided into two main types: in-line and off-line depaneling equipment. Off-line depaneling equipment, on the other hand, operates independently of the production line, allowing for batch processing and flexibility in handling various panel sizes and designs. Both types cater to different manufacturing needs, enhancing productivity and ensuring precise board separation.</p></p>
