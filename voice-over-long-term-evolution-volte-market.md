<p><strong>The Voice Over Long-Term Evolution (VoLTE) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Voice Over IP Multimedia Subsystem (VoIMS)</li><li>Circuit Switched Fall Back (CFSB)</li><li>Dual Radio/Simultaneous Voice and LTE (SVLTE)</li><li>Voice Over LTE Via Generic Access Network (VOLGA)</li><li>Single Radio Voice Call Continuity (SRVCC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Voice Over Long-Term Evolution (VoLTE) encompasses various market types for delivering voice services over LTE networks. Voice over IP Multimedia Subsystem (VoIMS) integrates voice with IP-based multimedia. Circuit Switched Fall Back (CSFB) uses legacy networks for calls when LTE is unavailable. Dual Radio/Simultaneous Voice and LTE (SVLTE) allows concurrent voice and data transmission on separate radio channels. Voice Over LTE via Generic Access Network (VoLGA) connects LTE with circuit-switched networks. Single Radio Voice Call Continuity (SRVCC) ensures seamless voice service during handovers between LTE and older networks.</p></p>
