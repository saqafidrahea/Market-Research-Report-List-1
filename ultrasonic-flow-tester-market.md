These devices are favored in various industries due to their non-intrusive nature, high accuracy, and ability to handle a wide range of fluid types. <p><strong>The Ultrasonic Flow Tester Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intrusive</li><li>Non-Intrusive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrasonic flow tester market is categorized into intrusive and non-intrusive types. Intrusive flow testers involve placing sensors directly in the fluid flow path, allowing for precise measurements of flow rates and characteristics, but may require pipeline modifications. Non-intrusive testers utilize external sensors to measure flow without altering the pipe system, providing a quick and easy installation with minimal disruption. Both types offer distinct advantages depending on application needs, with non-intrusive options generally favored for their convenience and reduced maintenance requirements.</p></p>
