<p><p>The bus air compressor market consists of several types, each serving unique applications. Rotary screw compressors are favored for their continuous operation and efficiency, making them ideal for commercial vehicles. Reciprocating air compressors are popular for their high-pressure output and versatile usage. Axial compressors are utilized in larger systems, offering high flow rates and efficiency at elevated speeds. Centrifugal compressors are known for their ability to handle large volumes of air efficiently, commonly used in high-capacity bus systems.</p></p>
