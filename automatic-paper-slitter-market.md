<p><ul><li>"Razor Blade"</li><li>"Shear Blade"</li><li>"Air Score Blade"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Paper Slitter market features three main types of cutting mechanisms: Razor Blade, Shear Blade, and Air Score Blade. Razor Blades offer precision cutting with minimal waste, ideal for thin materials. Shear Blades employ an upward and downward action, providing clean cuts and efficiency for thicker paper stocks. Air Score Blades utilize air pressure to create controlled perforations, suitable for products needing easy tearing. Each type caters to specific applications, enhancing production efficiency in the paper processing industry.</p></p>
