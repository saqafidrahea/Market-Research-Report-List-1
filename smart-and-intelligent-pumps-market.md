<p><p>The Smart and Intelligent Pumps market encompasses various types of pumps, notably centrifugal and positive displacement pumps. Centrifugal pumps utilize rotational energy from impellers to move fluids, making them ideal for high flow rates and low-viscosity fluids. In contrast, positive displacement pumps operate by trapping a fixed amount of fluid and forcing it through the discharge, providing consistent flow even against high pressures. Both types benefit from advancements in smart technology, enhancing efficiency, monitoring, and control capabilities in various industrial applications.</p></p>
