<p><p>The direct-operated solenoid valve market encompasses different types based on the number of ports and functions. A 2-way solenoid valve controls flow between two points, ideal for on/off applications. The 3-way valve can divert flow between two paths, often used in mixing or diverting applications. A 4-way solenoid valve manages air flow in pneumatic systems, enabling both supply and exhaust. The 5-way valve serves specific functions like reversing flow direction. Additionally, the "Other" category includes specialized designs for niche applications.</p></p>
