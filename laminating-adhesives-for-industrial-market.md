<p><p>Laminating adhesives for the industrial market are crucial for bonding materials in various applications. Solvent-based laminating adhesives contain solvents that facilitate bonding but can emit volatile organic compounds (VOCs), affecting environmental safety. Conversely, solventless laminating adhesives use reactive resins, offering a more environmentally friendly option with reduced VOC emissions and improved safety. These adhesives cater to diverse industries, including packaging, automotive, and electronics, providing flexibility and strength while enhancing product durability and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
