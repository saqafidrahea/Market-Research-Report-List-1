<p><strong>The Diamond Abrasives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sintering</li><li>Plating</li><li>Brazing</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diamond abrasives market can be categorized into four primary types: sintering, plating, brazing, and others. Sintering involves compacting diamond particles under high heat and pressure, creating durable tools. Plating refers to the application of a layer of diamond particles onto a metal substrate, enhancing cutting efficiency. Brazing bonds diamonds to a metal matrix using a filler metal, providing excellent strength. The "others" category encompasses various methods and applications, reflecting diverse technological advancements and market needs.</p></p>
