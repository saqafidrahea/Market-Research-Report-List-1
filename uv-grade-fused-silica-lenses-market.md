<p><p>The UV Grade Fused Silica Lenses market is categorized primarily into convex and concave lenses. Convex lenses are outward-curving and focus light to a point, making them ideal for applications like beam expansion and collimation. Conversely, concave lenses curve inward and disperse light, used for applications requiring beam divergence or reduced magnification. Both types offer high optical quality and durability, enabling their use in advanced optical systems such as laser technology, microscopy, and telecommunications.</p></p>
