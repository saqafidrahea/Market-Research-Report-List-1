<p><p>A Vacuum Pressure Impregnation System (VPIS) is used to enhance the durability and performance of porous materials, particularly in industries like automotive, aerospace, and electrical. This system works by drawing out air and other gases from the material under a vacuum, allowing resin or other impregnating fluids to fill the voids. The result is a stronger, more resistant component that can withstand harsh operating conditions.</p><p>The Vacuum Pressure Impregnation System Market is poised for significant growth, expected to expand at a CAGR of 10% during the forecast period.
