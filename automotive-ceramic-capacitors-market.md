<p><ul><li>Single Layer</li><li>Multiple-layer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive ceramic capacitors market is primarily categorized into single-layer and multiple-layer types. Single-layer capacitors are compact and designed for high-frequency applications, offering high reliability in limited space. In contrast, multiple-layer capacitors consist of several layers of ceramic dielectric and conductive materials, allowing for higher capacitance values in a smaller footprint, making them suitable for various automotive applications like power management and signal filtering. Both types enhance the performance and efficiency of electronic systems in vehicles.</p></p>
