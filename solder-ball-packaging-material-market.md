<p><strong>The Solder Ball Packaging Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lead Solder Ball</li><li>Lead Free Solder Ball</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solder ball packaging material market is primarily categorized into lead solder balls and lead-free solder balls. Lead solder balls contain lead as a primary alloying component, providing excellent conductivity and solderability, but raise health and environmental concerns. On the other hand, lead-free solder balls are formulated without lead, utilizing alternatives like tin, silver, and copper. These are increasingly preferred due to regulatory pressures and safer environmental profiles, especially in electronics manufacturing and assembly processes.</p></p>
