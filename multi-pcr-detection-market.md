<p><strong>The Multi-PCR Detection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Quantitative PCR Detection</li><li>Digital PCR Detection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Multi-PCR Detection market encompasses two primary types: Quantitative PCR (qPCR) and Digital PCR (dPCR). qPCR quantifies DNA by measuring the amplification of targeted sequences in real-time, allowing for precise measurement of nucleic acid concentrations. dPCR, on the other hand, offers absolute quantification by partitioning samples into thousands of individual reactions, providing heightened sensitivity and accuracy. Both techniques are pivotal in various applications, including clinical diagnostics, research, and pathogen detection, driving advancements in genomic analysis.</p></p>
