<p><p>The AL2O3 coated battery separator market primarily consists of two types: PP (polypropylene) type and PE (polyethylene) type. PP type separators are known for their high thermal stability and chemical resistance, making them suitable for high-performance lithium-ion batteries. In contrast, PE type separators are valued for their excellent electrolyte wettability and flexibility, enhancing ion conductivity. Both types feature AL2O3 coatings, which improve mechanical strength and reduce dendrite growth, ultimately enhancing battery safety and lifespan in various applications.</p></p>
