<p><ul><li>Calcined Ceria Slurry</li><li>Colloidal Ceria Slurry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ceria CMP (Chemical Mechanical Planarization) Slurry market is primarily divided into two types: calcined ceria slurry and colloidal ceria slurry. Calcined ceria slurry is produced from thermally treated cerium oxide, offering enhanced polishing efficiency and reduced defects on substrates. In contrast, colloidal ceria slurry consists of stable, fine cerium oxide particles dispersed in a liquid medium, providing superior surface finish and uniformity. Both types play crucial roles in semiconductor manufacturing and precision optics polishing, catering to different processing needs.</p></p>
