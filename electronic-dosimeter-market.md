<p><p>The electronic dosimeter market is categorized into three main types: TLD dosimeters, OSL dosimeters, and others. TLD (Thermoluminescent Dosimeters) utilize materials that store energy from radiation exposure and release it as light when heated, allowing for accurate dose measurement. OSL (Optically Stimulated Luminescence) dosimeters work similarly, using light stimulation to read accumulated radiation exposure. Other types may include film badge dosimeters or electronic personal dosimeters, each offering unique detection capabilities and applications in radiation monitoring and safety.</p></p>
