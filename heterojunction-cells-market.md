<p><p>Heterojunction cells, also known as HJT cells, are advanced solar photovoltaic technologies that combine amorphous and crystalline silicon layers to enhance efficiency and performance. Companies are also focusing on integrating HJT technology into larger photovoltaic systems to maximize energy output.
