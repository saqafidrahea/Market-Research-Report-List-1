<p><ul><li>Linear Encoder ICs</li><li>Rotary Encoder ICs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The magnetic encoder IC market comprises two primary types: linear encoder ICs and rotary encoder ICs. Linear encoder ICs are designed to measure position along a straight path, enabling precise control in applications like industrial automation and robotics. In contrast, rotary encoder ICs are used to determine angular position or rotation, making them essential for applications such as motor control and position tracking in various devices. Both types leverage magnetic sensing technology to provide accurate and reliable measurements.</p></p>
