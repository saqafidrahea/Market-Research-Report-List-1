<p><p>The raw meat speciation testing market encompasses various methods to identify meat species accurately. PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) is a molecular technique that amplifies specific DNA sequences, providing precise species identification. ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) detects proteins related to specific species in meat samples. Other molecular diagnostic tests, such as LC-MS/MS (Liquid Chromatography-Tandem Mass Spectrometry), analyze complex samples for species-specific markers. These testing methods are crucial for ensuring food safety, preventing fraud, and maintaining consumer trust in meat products.</p></p>
