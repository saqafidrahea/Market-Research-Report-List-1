<p><p>The radiation protection glasses market includes two main types of lenses: plated film lenses and synthesis film lenses. Plated film lenses feature a thin layer of protective material applied onto the lens surface, effectively reducing radiation exposure while maintaining visibility. In contrast, synthesis film lenses are made from advanced materials that inherently block harmful radiation without additional coatings. Both lens types cater to various industries and applications, ensuring user safety while offering comfort and visual clarity in environments with potential radiation hazards.</p></p>
