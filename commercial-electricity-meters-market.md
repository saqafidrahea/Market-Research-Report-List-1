<p><p>The Commercial Electricity Meters market comprises primarily two types: Single Phase and Three-Phase electricity meters. Single Phase meters cater to smaller commercial operations, typically serving low to moderate power requirements, ideal for retail shops and small offices. Conversely, Three-Phase meters are designed for larger commercial and industrial businesses, providing higher power capacities necessary for heavy machinery and complex electrical systems. Both meter types play a crucial role in energy management, billing, and monitoring electricity consumption for various business applications.</p></p>
