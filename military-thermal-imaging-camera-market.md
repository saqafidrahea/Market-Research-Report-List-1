<p><ul><li>Passive</li><li>Initiative</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The military thermal imaging camera market is primarily divided into two types: passive and active systems. Passive thermal imaging cameras detect naturally emitted infrared radiation from objects, providing a stealthy means of surveillance without emitting signals. In contrast, active thermal imaging systems illuminate a target with infrared light, reflecting the energy off the object to create an image. Both types contribute significantly to situational awareness in military operations, enhancing target identification and tracking capabilities across various environments.</p></p>
