<p><ul><li>Power-Assisted Steering (EPAS)</li><li>Electric Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Steering Control Module market primarily consists of Power-Assisted Steering (EPAS) and Electric Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS) systems. EPAS utilizes electric motors to assist steering effort, enhancing fuel efficiency and reducing vehicle weight. Conversely, EHPS combines hydraulic systems with electric assistance, offering improved responsiveness and control while maintaining the benefits of hydraulic power. Both technologies are vital for modern vehicles, providing enhanced maneuverability, safety, and driver comfort while contributing to the overall efficiency of the steering system.</p></p>
