<p><p>The hematology drugs market includes several key types that address various blood-related conditions. Cyclooxygenase inhibitors reduce inflammation and pain by blocking enzymes involved in prostaglandin production. Antiplatelet agents prevent blood clots by inhibiting platelet aggregation, crucial for managing cardiovascular diseases. Thrombin inhibitors directly block thrombin in the coagulation cascade, helping prevent thromboembolic events. Demethylating agents are used in cancer treatment to reverse abnormal gene silencing, aiding in the restoration of normal blood cell function. Together, these categories play vital roles in hematological therapies.</p></p>
