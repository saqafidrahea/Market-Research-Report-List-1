<p><p>The 5G RF switches market is categorized into four main types: PIN diodes, GaAs (Gallium Arsenide), SOI (Silicon On Insulator) and SOS (Silicon On Sapphire), and MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems). </p><p>PIN diodes offer fast switching and high power handling, ideal for RF applications. GaAs provides high efficiency and low noise, suitable for advanced 5G systems. SOI and SOS technologies allow for better isolation and performance in integrated circuits. MEMS switches deliver low insertion loss and compact size, enhancing system miniaturization and performance.</p></p>
