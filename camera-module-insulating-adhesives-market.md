<p><p>The Camera Module Insulating Adhesives Market can be categorized into two main types: UV Curable Type and Non-Curable Type. UV Curable Adhesives are formulated to cure quickly when exposed to ultraviolet light, providing strong bonds and effective insulation for camera modules. In contrast, Non-Curable Adhesives do not rely on UV light for curing, offering flexibility in application but often requiring longer setting times. Both types are essential for enhancing the performance and durability of camera modules in various electronic devices.</p></p>
