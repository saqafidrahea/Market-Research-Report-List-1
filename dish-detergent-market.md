<p><p>The dish detergent market comprises three main types: hand washing products, automatic dishwashing products, and rinsing agents. Hand washing products include liquid soaps and gels designed for washing dishes by hand, often focusing on grease-cutting capabilities and skin sensitivity. Automatic dishwashing products, such as tablets and powders, are formulated for use in dishwashers, providing effective cleaning at high temperatures. Rinsing agents enhance the drying process and prevent water spots, ensuring dishes come out sparkling clean after washing.</p></p>
