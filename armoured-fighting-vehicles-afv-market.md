These vehicles enhance protection by actively responding to incoming attacks. In contrast, passive armored vehicles rely on physical armor plating for protection, utilizing materials like steel and composite armor to withstand impacts. Both types serve diverse roles in military operations, emphasizing survivability and tactical effectiveness on the battlefield.</p></p>
