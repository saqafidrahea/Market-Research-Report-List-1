<p><p>The hypochlorous acid market comprises primarily two types: sodium hypochlorite and calcium hypochlorite. Sodium hypochlorite, a solution of chlorine, is widely used for water disinfection, bleaching, and cleaning applications. Calcium hypochlorite, a solid compound, is favored for pool sanitation and water treatment due to its stability and effectiveness in producing chlorine upon dissolution. Both types are essential for maintaining hygiene and public health, finding applications across municipal, agricultural, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
