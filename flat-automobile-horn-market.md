<p><ul><li>Electronic Horn</li><li>Electromagnetic Horns</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flat automobile horn market encompasses two primary types: electronic horns and electromagnetic horns. Electronic horns utilize integrated circuits to produce sound, offering a range of tones and often featuring more compact designs. In contrast, electromagnetic horns rely on a mechanical diaphragm activated by electromagnetic forces, generating a traditional, robust sound. Both types serve the same purpose of alerting pedestrians and vehicles, but they differ in technology, sound quality, size, and installation requirements, catering to diverse consumer preferences and automotive designs.</p></p>
